FAQ

How to participate in BNBPrime?

BNBPrime operates on the Binance Smart Chain and to interact with the contract you need to install the Metamask browser extension (an identical mobile application is also available for IPhone and Android users).

How to invest in BNBPrime?

Chose the desired Deposit Period for you investment, enter the amount of BNB you'd like to deposit and click invest. That's it.

What is the difference between different Deposit Periods?

The Deposit Period can be from 7 to 30 days. Keep in mind that different Deposit Periods have different profitability between 119% and 234%. The longer your Deposit Period - the bigger the reward.

Can I have multiple deposits?

Yes,sure! The number of your deposits is not limited.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes, our Referral Program features 5 levels of referral rewards. For the friends that you invite you receive % of their deposits. Check the persentages for each level on our website.
